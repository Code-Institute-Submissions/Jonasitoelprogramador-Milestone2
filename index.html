About

Welcome to City Search! This brand-new application revolutionises the way we find our favourite activities and places whithin a city. Simply type in your city and the activity or place you would like to do/visit and City Search will return the five highest-rated results for this keyword on Google Maps reviews. Click on any of the markers to reveal the place name, adress and rating and off you go! City Search also prioritises by number of reviews and discards any places that have not received enough reviews to be considered reliable. Enjoy!
